About Me

Hello! I'm Magda Rajszewski, a dedicated student leader and Eagle Scout from Houston, Texas. I'm passionate about creating positive change through leadership, service, and continuous learning.

As an IB Diploma Candidate at Lamar High School, I balance rigorous academics with extensive extracurricular involvement. From founding the Leaders of Tomorrow organization to serving as Senior Patrol Leader in Boy Scouts, I believe in leading by example and empowering others to reach their full potential.

My journey has taken me from the Swiss Alps on a challenging backpacking expedition to St. Paul's Cathedral in London as Head Chorister, from Texas Girls State where I served as Mayor to West Point's SLE program. Each experience has shaped my commitment to excellence, service, and leadership.

Beyond academics and leadership, I'm driven by a love for running, music, and community service. Whether I'm leading my cross country team, performing in musical productions, or coordinating kindness initiatives through Be.Kinder.You, I strive to make every moment count.
